Instructions for Using the Map Application


Overview

Welcome to our interactive map application! This guide will help you make the most out of your mapping experience. Below are step-by-step instructions on how to navigate the map, utilize its features, and explore different functionalities.

Getting Started

Zoom In and Out:
Use the "+" and "-" buttons or scroll with your mouse wheel to zoom in and out on the map.
Pan:
Click and drag the map to move around and explore different areas.
Zoom to Layer Extent:
Click the "Zoom to Layer Extent" button to fit the map view to the extent of the base layer.
Advanced Features:
Navigate History:
Use the "Previous Extent" and "Next Extent" buttons to navigate through previously visited extents.
Add Bookmarks:
Click the "Add Bookmark" button to save the current map extent for quick access later. You can manage bookmarks from the dropdown menu.
Explore Layers:
Toggle different layers using the layer switcher to view additional information such as satellite imagery, terrain, or other thematic layers.

Keyboard Shortcuts:

Use keyboard shortcuts (if supported) for zooming in and out ("+" and "-"), panning (arrow keys), and other common interactions.
Help and Support:
For further assistance or questions, please refer to the user manual or contact our support team at support@example.com.

Now that you're familiar with the map application, feel free to explore and discover new places with ease. Enjoy your journey!
